Why the Fold?

The flaw with traditional yoga mats: germs from the floor side of the mat roll right onto the practice side of the mat.

The Koru Solution

Koru Mat is a 6mm high-quality yoga mat (SGS Tested, 7P Free) with an embossed fold that keeps your practice side off the floor.

Fold first, then roll: the clean side never touches the ground and pack-up takes half the time.
